Service 02

YouTube likeness enforcement

YouTube's likeness rights policy — introduced for AI-generated content — gives us a powerful lever that most generalist DMCA services don't use. We file specifically under this clause when your face, voice, or persona has been used without consent in synthetic video.

Beyond individual videos, we pursue the channels themselves through YouTube's repeat-infringer termination framework. Prolific impersonator accounts are removed at the root — not just video by video.

Service 03

Social impersonation & domain takedowns

Fake accounts on Instagram, Facebook, X, and TikTok — combined with fraudulent domains — create complete phishing ecosystems. We dismantle each layer: social account suspension filings, registrar abuse complaints, and hosting provider notices, coordinated simultaneously.

Domain takedowns use ICANN's Uniform Domain-Name Dispute-Resolution Policy (UDRP) where appropriate, and direct registrar abuse channels for faster cases. We also issue DMCA takedowns to hosting providers when the infringing content is on a custom site.
